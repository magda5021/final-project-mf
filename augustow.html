Augustow

Augustow is my home town and it is a resort town in northeastern Poland. It is known for its therapeutic spas. Flanked by lakes, the town also borders the Augustow Primeval Forest. At the junction of the river and the Augustow Canal, the Augustow Canal Museum traces the waterway's 19th-century origins as a trade route. Forests and wetlands covers Biebrza National Park to the south and Wigry National Park north of town. One of the traditional dishes are kartacze and potato babka. One of the popular regional cakes are sekacz and mrowisko. Augustow is a perfect place for kayaking, sailing, water skiing or hiking. In 2014 Augustow was honored as a "Nicest City in Poland" and video was made to promote Augustow. Click on the link to watch the video. Augustow-Milka

    • 2. Szuflada Bistro

    Bistro Szuflada is a place for those who are looking for the kitchen based on local products like cheese made in Korycin, meats, silages and preserves made by the owners. They specialize in a healthy approach to the kitchen. Their homemade drinks are interesting combinations of flavors of herbs and even vegetables. Beer lovers will also find their shelf in the fridge, they have a large selection of craft and regional bottles of beers from all over Poland. In the evenings, mainly on the lower hall in the building, they often organize culture events for our guests. Concerts, stand-ups and theater performances take place in an intimate environment.
